Minecraft Server Rules:

1. KEEP IT WHOLESOME

  • No adult / NSFW content. Anywhere.
  • No swearing, we have content creators recording.
  • Talk to people how you’d want to be talked to.

2. DON'T BE A WRECKING BALL

  • Don’t destroy, deface, or steal from other players’ builds or storage.
  • Don’t leave craters, junk or half-finished mess in active areas.
  • No lag machines, obviously.

3. NO CHEATING NO EXCUSES

  • This includes hacking, general item duplication (duping), and abusing game exploits.
  • Play minigames as intended. Don’t tamper with them.
  • X-ray or similar texture packs or mods are not allowed.
  • “Freecam” is allowed if it gives you no unfair advantage.
  • Duping Falling Blocks is not allowed.
  • TNT duping is allowed if your farm actually needs it.
  • Duping rails, carpets, tripwire hooks and string is allowed if proper item collection is in place.

4. BE CONSIDERATE

  • In voice chat, sort your mic: no echo, no screech.
  • Don’t kill people for no reason.
  • Every farm and redstone build must have an on/off switch.
  • Don't have every farm loaded at all times, go to them when you need them.
  • Try to make ugly farms look less painful.
  • Spawn-proof the Nether roof.
  • Please use community Villager Trading rather than making your own.
  • Don't rob The End blind forcing new players to go for miles to get an Elytra.

5. TAKE IT EASY!

  • Pranks must be in good taste and cleaned up after.
  • Player “wars” are role-play only.
  • The Ender Dragon Egg is always stealable, you can hide it in your base but it must be discoverable without breaking blocks or opening chests.
  • If someone doesn’t want to join in, respect it.
  • Advertising other Discord or Minecraft servers is forbidden.
